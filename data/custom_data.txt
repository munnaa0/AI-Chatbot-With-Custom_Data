Academic Programs and Curriculum

The primary offering of the MCT department is the Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Multimedia and Creative Technology. This program encompasses a diverse curriculum that includes digital media, animation, game development, graphic design, and interactive technology. The courses are designed to be hands-on, ensuring that students acquire practical experience alongside theoretical knowledge. ​

Facilities and Resources

The MCT department is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities to support student learning and creativity. Notably, it offers labs dedicated to augmented reality (AR), virtual reality (VR), and motion capture technology, providing students with access to cutting-edge tools and environments. Additionally, the department has established a business wing named Spiral World, a licensed entity under the Daffodil Family, which offers students opportunities to gain industry experience and engage in entrepreneurial ventures. ​

Vision and Mission

The MCT department is committed to fostering a blend of creativity and technical expertise among its students. By providing a curriculum that emphasizes both artistic expression and technological skills, the department aims to prepare graduates who are well-equipped to excel in the rapidly evolving fields of multimedia and creative technology. This approach ensures that students are not only proficient in current technologies but also adaptable to future innovations in the industry. ​

In summary, the Department of Multimedia and Creative Technology at Daffodil International University offers a robust program that integrates creative arts with technological training. With experienced faculty, advanced facilities, and a comprehensive curriculum, the department provides students with the necessary tools and opportunities to thrive in the dynamic field of multimedia and creative technology.
